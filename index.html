Explore India like never before

Backwaters of Kerela

The scenic backwaters of Kerala comprise serene stretches of lakes, canals and lagoons located parallel to the coast of the Arabian Sea. Backwater regions of Kerala are one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world.

Ghats of Varanasi

Every ghat of Varanasi has its own story. Ghats in Varanasi are riverfront steps leading to the banks of the Ganges river. The city has 88 ghats. Most of the ghats are bathing and puja ceremonial ghats.

Forts of Rajasthan

The most fascinating aspect about Rajasthan is its forts. These huge structures are testimonies to the splendour Rajasthan has witnessed all through history. They take you back to the time of kings and queens, commoners and ministers.

Mountains of Uttarakhand

The beauty, weather, and culture of Uttarakhand are totally different and unique.It attracts tourist with its beautiful views and fresh snow. You can enjoy adventure activities such as skiing, paragliding, snow scooter and more.

Beaches of Goa

Goa is a paradise where water is turquoise, the beaches have soft white sands and sky changes colour of different hues. It is one of the finest beach destinations of the world where you can find pristine beaches and a laid-back lifestyle.

Tea Gardens of Assam

The tea gardens in Assam are some of the most beautiful spots for tourists. They are lush green and very pleasing to the eyes. This brew provides a number of people with a means to earn their livelihood.
